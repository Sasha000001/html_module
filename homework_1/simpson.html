The Simpsons

The Simpsons Movie is the First Feature Length film based on The Simpsons. It is produced by Gracie Films for 20th Century Fox with animation produced by Film Roman and Rough Draft Studios and was released worldwide July 27, 2007 just after 18 seasons.

Homer Jay Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson (born May 12, 1956) is the main protagonist of The Simpsons series. He is the spouse of Marge Simpson and father of Bart, Lisa and Maggie Simpson. Homer is overweight (said to be ~240 pounds), lazy, and often ignorant to the world around him.

  • “D'oh!” ― Homer when he makes a mistake.
  • “Woo-hoo!” ― Homer when something good happens to him.

Bart Simpson

Bartholomew "Bart" Jojo Simpson (born April 1[7] or February 23[8]) is the mischievous, rebellious, misunderstood, disruptive and "potentially dangerous" oldest child of the Simpson family in The Simpsons. He is the only son of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the older brother of Lisa and Maggie. He also has been nicknamed "Cosmo", after discovering a comet in "Bart's Comet".

  • “Ay Caramba!” ― Bart's catchphrase and first words
  • “Eat my shorts!” ―Bart's second catchphrase

Lisa Simpson

Lisa Marie Simpson (born May 9) is the elder daughter and middle child of the Simpson family and one of the two tritagonists (along with Marge,) of The Simpsons series.

  • “BAAAAART!!” ― Lisa’s catchphrase
  • “If anyone wants me, I'll be in my room.” ― Lisa's "catchphrase"
